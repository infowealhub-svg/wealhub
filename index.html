Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At WealHub, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy for any reason, we offer a straightforward return and refund process.

7-Day Return Window

You may return any item within 7 days of receiving it, provided the item is in its original condition and packaging. To initiate a return, contact us on WhatsApp at 0708 398 311 or email info.wealhub@gmail.com.

Eligibility for a Refund

  • Item must be unused and in its original packaging
  • Item must be returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation or WhatsApp chat) is required
  • Items damaged due to misuse are not eligible

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you within 1–2 business days. Approved refunds are processed via M-Pesa or the original payment method within 3–5 business days.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Software licences once activated
  • Items clearly damaged by the customer
  • Items returned after the 7-day window

Exchanges

We are happy to exchange items for a different model or product of equal value. Contact us on WhatsApp to arrange an exchange.

Shipping Policy

Last updated: May 2026

WealHub is committed to getting your order to you as quickly as possible. Here's everything you need to know about how we deliver.

Delivery Areas

We currently offer delivery across Nairobi and its environs. For orders outside Nairobi, please contact us on WhatsApp to discuss options and costs.

Same-Day Delivery

Orders placed before 2:00 PM EAT (Monday – Friday) are eligible for same-day delivery within Nairobi. Orders placed after 2PM or on weekends will be delivered the next business day.

Delivery Times

  • Nairobi CBD & Westlands: 1–3 hours
  • Nairobi suburbs (Karen, Kilimani, Eastleigh, etc.): 2–5 hours
  • Outskirts of Nairobi: 4–8 hours or next day

Delivery Fees

Delivery fees vary by location and order size. We will confirm the exact delivery fee when you place your order on WhatsApp. For large orders, delivery may be free — ask us!

Order Tracking

All orders are tracked via WhatsApp. You will receive updates when your order is confirmed, dispatched, and out for delivery. Our rider will call you approximately 30 minutes before arrival.

Damaged or Lost Orders

In the unlikely event your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact us immediately on WhatsApp. We will resolve the issue at no extra cost to you.
