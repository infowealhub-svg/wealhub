Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At WealHub, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

7-Day Return Window

You may return any item within 7 days of receiving it, in its original condition. Contact us on WhatsApp at 0708 398 311 or email info.wealhub@gmail.com to initiate a return.

Eligibility

  • Item unused and in original packaging
  • Returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Proof of purchase required
  • Items damaged by misuse are not eligible

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the item, we notify you within 1–2 business days. Approved refunds are processed via M-Pesa within 3–5 business days.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Software licences once activated
  • Items clearly damaged by the customer
  • Items returned after the 7-day window

Shipping Policy

Last updated: May 2026

WealHub is committed to getting your order to you as quickly as possible.

Delivery Areas

We deliver across Nairobi and its environs. For orders outside Nairobi, contact us on WhatsApp.

Same-Day Delivery

Orders placed before 2:00 PM EAT (Monday – Friday) are eligible for same-day delivery. Orders after 2PM or on weekends are delivered the next business day.

Delivery Times

All orders are tracked via WhatsApp. Our rider will call you approximately 30 minutes before arrival.
